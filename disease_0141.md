# Superficial Spreading Melanoma

## Basic Information

- **ID**: disease_0141
- **Category**: cancer
- **Parent**: skin_cancer
- **Type**: subtype

## Clinical Information

- **Prevalence**: 70% of melanomas
- **Characteristics**: Horizontal growth phase before vertical invasion
- **Prognosis**: Better than nodular melanoma
- **Common Sites**: Trunk (men), Legs (women)
- **Age Group**: Middle-aged adults
- **Risk Factors**: ['UV exposure', 'Fair skin', 'Multiple nevi']
